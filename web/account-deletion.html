BetterSpent — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: April 16, 2026

You do NOT need to contact us to delete your account.
Deleting your account in the BetterSpent app will delete your account and associated data.

How to delete your account (in-app)

1) Open the BetterSpent app
2) Go to Settings → Account
3) Tap Manage
4) And then tap Delete Account and confirm

If you can’t access the app

Email us at: 6731503057@lamduan.mfu.ac.th
Send the request from the email address associated with your BetterSpent account.
Subject: Delete my account

Data that is deleted

Your Firebase Authentication user account (sign-in credentials).
Your app data stored in our database, including:
Expense entries (amount, category, date, note).
Financial profile (income, monthly budget).
Settings (currency, feature toggles/preferences).
Local cached copies of the above on your device (cleared as part of the deletion flow).

Data that may be kept

Operational/security logs (for example, troubleshooting and abuse prevention). These may contain technical identifiers and may be retained for up to 30 days.
Aggregated, non-identifying metrics (if any) that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.
