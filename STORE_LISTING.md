**Master Your Money with BetterSpent**

Take control of your finances with BetterSpent, the intuitive expense tracking app designed to help you understand and optimize your spending habits. Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone looking to gain better control over your finances, BetterSpent makes expense tracking simple and efficient.

**Key Features:**

💬 **Smart Expense Entry** - Add expenses lightning-fast using natural language input. Simply type "coffee 5.50" and our smart parser automatically categorizes and logs your spending in seconds. No complex forms or unnecessary steps.

📊 **Comprehensive Dashboard** - Get an at-a-glance overview of your financial health. View your current balance, total income, monthly budget, and daily spending limits all in one place. Stay informed about your financial status instantly.

📋 **Detailed Expense Management** - Keep complete records of all your spending. View expenses grouped by date, easily edit or delete entries, and maintain a full transaction history. Your financial records, organized and accessible.

💡 **Insightful Summaries** - Understand your spending patterns with weekly and monthly summaries. See your total spending, daily averages, and detailed breakdowns by category. Identify trends and make smarter decisions about your money.

💰 **Budget & Income Tracking** - Set a monthly budget and track your income to stay in control of your finances. Monitor your progress throughout the month and adjust spending as needed to meet your financial goals.

⚙️ **Customizable Settings** - Tailor the app to your preferences. Choose your preferred currency, enable or disable smart input mode, manage notifications, and enjoy a dark mode UI for comfortable viewing anytime.

🌙 **Dark Mode UI** - Enjoy a clean, modern interface designed for comfortable viewing day or night. The sleek dark mode UI reduces eye strain while keeping all your financial information clear and accessible.

**Why Choose BetterSpent?**

BetterSpent cuts through the complexity of personal finance management with a straightforward, elegant approach. We focus on what matters most: helping you understand where your money goes so you can make intentional financial decisions. No confusing menus, no overwhelming features—just powerful insights delivered clearly.

The key to better spending habits is awareness. BetterSpent makes it effortless to track your expenses and review your patterns, giving you the visibility you need to optimize your budget and achieve your financial goals.

**Your Financial Awareness Starts Here**

Download BetterSpent today and take the first step toward mastering your spending habits. Whether you're working toward a savings goal, managing a tight budget, or simply want to understand your finances better, BetterSpent is here to help.
